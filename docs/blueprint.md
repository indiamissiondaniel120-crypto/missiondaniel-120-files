# **App Name**: DANIEL 120

## Core Features:

- Student Account Management: Enable secure registration and login for students to access personalized educational content.
- Course Selection Dashboard: Allow students to easily browse and select from various educational streams including classes 9, 10, 11, 12, NEET, and JEE.
- PDF Notes Access: Provide a section for each selected course where students can view and download essential study notes in PDF format.
- Video Lecture Playback: Offer streaming video lessons, uploaded to Firebase Storage, categorized by selected course, for an interactive learning experience.
- AI Study Companion Tool: Integrate an AI tool to assist students with concept understanding, provide summaries, or generate practice questions based on their selected course topics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo/Violet (#5E1FBA) to convey wisdom, focus, and a sense of depth, aligning with the pursuit of knowledge. This choice evokes intellectual calm and respect for learning.
- Background color: Soft Lavender White (#F3F1F8). This heavily desaturated hue of the primary provides a clean, inviting, and peaceful study environment that aids in concentration.
- Accent color: Bright Sky Blue (#1263FF). This analogous hue offers strong contrast, drawing attention to calls-to-action and important information, while symbolizing inspiration and clarity.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for its excellent readability across all devices and its balanced, warm, yet professional feel, supporting the app's educational and uplifting vision.
- Utilize clean, outline-style icons that are intuitive and relevant to educational contexts (e.g., a book for notes, a play button for videos, a graduation cap for classes). These icons should enhance navigation without visual clutter.
- Implement a clear, responsive layout that prioritizes readability and ease of navigation. Content sections should be well-defined, and important information, such as course selection and material access, should be immediately apparent.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations such as smooth transitions between sections and clear loading indicators for videos and PDFs. These should improve user experience without causing distractions.
